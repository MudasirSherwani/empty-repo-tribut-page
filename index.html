Michael Jackson

Michael Jackson began life in a working-class suburb of Gary, Indiana. Naturally musical, he joined his brothers in a band that would take the locals - and later the world - by storm 29 August 1958, Michael Joseph Jackson was born into a large, working-class family in the small, suburban town of Gary, Indiana, in the US. Baby Michael was the seventh child of Joseph and Katherine Jackson
